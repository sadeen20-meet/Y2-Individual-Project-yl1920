what is "cukur"?


çukur is a Turkish series set in one of the most dangerous neighborhoods of Istanbul.

This neighborhood is called çukur (the Pit)

This dangerous neighborhood is ran by a noble mafia family called Koçovaly.

When the family is in the danger of losing the control of The Pit.

their youngest son now must come back to his home, where he could never escape from.

Every man who lives their has ameaningful sign tattooed on their body

the thrills start to rise and take control of you ! every episode is thrilling ,

filled with emotions and extremely interesting events !

the way this series is moving is so special and unique ,the story is one of a kind and worth your time!
